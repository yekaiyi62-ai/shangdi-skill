# 雅思备考团队 · 示例

> 5个专业教练 + 1个协调器 + 共享进度追踪

## 团队成员

| 教练 | 核心能力 | 输入 → 输出 |
|------|---------|------------|
| **口语教练** | Part 1/2/3模拟、4维度评分 | 话题回答 → 评分卡 + 改进建议 + 高分替换 |
| **写作教练** | Task 1/2评分、逐句修改 | 作文文本 → 评分卡 + diff修改 + 范文 |
| **听力训练师** | 题型攻略、精听方案 | 薄弱描述 → 诊断 + 训练方案 + 材料推荐 |
| **词汇监督** | 话题词汇、搭配训练、测试 | 话题/请求 → 词汇表 / 测试题 / 深度卡 |
| **学习规划师** | 制定计划、追踪进度 | 目标+时间 → 阶段化计划 + 每日任务 |
| **协调器** | 分发任务、汇总报告 | 任意输入 → 转给对应教练 / 综合报告 |

## 效果示例对话

### 对话1：写作教练评分

```
用户      ❯ Some people believe that children should spend all their free 
            time studying. Others think they should have more leisure 
            activities. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

            In today's competitive world, education plays a very important 
            role. Some people think children should study all the time, while 
            others believe leisure activities are also important. In my 
            opinion, children need a balance between study and play.

            On the one hand, studying is very good for children because 
            they can learn many things. If children study hard, they will 
            get good grades and find good jobs in the future. For example, 
            in China, many parents want their children to study very hard 
            because education is very important for success.

            On the other hand, leisure activities are also important for 
            children. Playing sports can make children healthy and strong. 
            Also, doing art or music can help children be more creative. 
            Furthermore, playing with friends can teach children social skills.

            In conclusion, I believe children should have both study time 
            and leisure time. Parents should help children find a good 
            balance between the two.

写作教练   ❯ ═══════════════════════════════
             写作评分 · Task 2
             题目：Children study vs leisure
             字数：178 词
            ═══════════════════════════════

            📊 分数：5.5 / 9.0

            维度细分：
              TA  任务完成    6.0  两面讨论+立场都有，但论证浅
              CC  连贯衔接    5.5  有连接词但过度依赖On the one hand
              LR  词汇资源    5.0  "very important/good"重复7次
              GRA 语法准确    6.0  句式简单但基本准确

            📝 逐段反馈：

            【开头段】
              ❌ "In today's competitive world" → 套话，直接改写题目
              ❌ "very important role" → "a pivotal role in shaping 
                 children's futures"
              ⚡ 可以更直接表明立场

            【主体段1】
              ❌ "very good for children" → "academically beneficial"
              ❌ "learn many things" → "acquire essential knowledge 
                 and skills"
              ⚠️ 只有一个例子（中国家长），需要更具体的论证

            【主体段2】
              ⚡ 结构不错，但每个点都只是一句话
              ❌ "make children healthy and strong" → "promote 
                 physical well-being"
              ⚡ 加一个具体例子会提升TA分数

            【结尾】
              ⚠️ 只是重复了开头，没有总结性见解
              ⚡ 加一句"how to balance"会更有力

            ⚠️ 字数不足！Task 2要求250+词，你只写了178词。
               字数不够会被扣分，这是最容易修复的问题。

            📊 总结：
              最强维度：GRA（语法基本准确）
              最弱维度：LR（词汇太重复）
              提分关键：
              1. 先把字数写够250+
              2. 消灭"very+简单形容词"，用话题词汇替换
              3. 每个论点加具体例子或数据

            需要看同题目的7.0范文吗？
```

### 对话3：词汇监督出题

```
用户      ❯ 考我一下教育相关的词汇

词汇监督   ❯ 来！10道题，混合题型，3分钟完成：

            【英译中】
            1. curriculum → ___
            2. tuition fees → ___
            3. extracurricular → ___

            【搭配选择】
            4. ___ an exam (take / make / do)
            5. ___ knowledge (gain / earn / win)
            6. academic ___ (result / achievement / score)

            【语境填空】
            7. Many universities offer ___ to students from 
               low-income families. (奖学金)
            8. The ___ rate in this country has increased 
               significantly. (识字率/文化水平)

            【近义词辨析】
            9. "learn" vs "acquire": 哪个更正式？
            10. "teacher" vs "educator" vs "instructor": 
                雅思写作中用哪个最好？

            直接打答案，我来批改。
```
